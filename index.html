Butter Chicken Recipe

A traditional, easy, and delicious meal.

Preparation

50 Min

Cook

25 Min

Ready In

60 Min

Ingredients

  • 2 tablespoon chicken fat
  • 2 tablespoon reserved chicken
  • 4 tablespoon sriracha chili sauce
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 teaspoon ginger, finely minced
  • 1 1/2 cups reserved chicken
  • 1/2 teaspoon sesame oil
  • 1 whole chicken
  • 1 1/2 cups chopped onion

Directions

01. Mix and stir the dough to 70% then add butter. Stir the dough again until evenly combined. Leave the dough to ferment between 26-28, ferment for 90 minutes at 26-28. Divide the dough into 80g portions.

02. Moulding the dough: cut into desired shapes and coat them with oil. Roll once more.

03. Rest the dough at 36-38 and humidity 75-80% for 1 hour. Ferment the dough to 70%. After baking, temper the dough before flame-off at 80g per piece.
